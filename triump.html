The Build

The case build is nothing short of spectacular combining Titanium with 316L Stainless Steel. We designed the case to be Triumphantly strong and bold at 44mm in diameter and 17mm thick with deep tactile knurling on the case sides and crown. Then we layered the entire case in IP stealth matte black. We capped it with a lustrous Titanium bezel which has the highest strength to density ratio of any metal- period, making virtually scratch proof.

The Engine

We worked directly with Ronda, one of the most respected watch movement companies in Switzerland in choosing the coveted 3540D engine that powers the Triumph and sealed it securely with a completely screwed down caseback that affords the timepiece an astounding 660 feet water resistance.

Perfection

Function and form are synergistically blended into the Triumph via a beautifully curved case, lugs and caseback. In addition, we employed a unique process that allowed us the ability to create a complete integration of the genuine leatherstrap into the case on both the upper and lower lugs. The lower strap actually appears to be built into the watch body as it flows beneath the case and lugs.
